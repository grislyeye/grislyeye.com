For centuries the ratfolk have lurked in the sewers and catacombs of the Undercity, unbeknownst to the citizens above. But soon their wretched existence may be revealed...

The Vermin introduces the ratfolk, a new threat for 1st-4th level characters. This supplement includes a detailed description of the ratfolk with new monster stats and adventure seeds.

Includes original artwork by George Howard and Daniel Walthall.
