For centuries the ratfolk have lurked in the sewers and catacombs of the Undercity, unbeknownst to the citizens above. But soon their wretched existence may be revealed...

The Vermin introduces the ratfolk, a new monster for 1st-4th level characters. This 11 page supplement includes a detailed description of the ratfolk with illustrations, 4 new monster stats (CR 1/4 to 1), adventure seeds and an example ratfolk nest.

Includes original artwork by George Howard and Daniel Walthall, and cartography by Dyson Logos.
