CONT/EXT is a Mothership one-shot of existential horror. A britpunk nightmare set on the Lachesis: a failing, deep-space research station.

The Lachesis has gone radio silent, and it's up to your crew to investigate why, and recover as much valuable research as possible. (Rescue optional).

Current Status: In playtest. Cover and writing are done, and I've started on some preliminary design and layout work.
