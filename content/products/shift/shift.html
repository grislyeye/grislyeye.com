Status: At Concept

Being a teenager on a zero-hour contract is tough, but then you get stuck in a remote gas station during a snowstorm. Just as shit starts to get weird.

Can you weather the night, or are you just another casualty of the gig economy?

An adventure for Liminal Horror and Liminal High School, in the genre of gig work horror.

It takes place in the Eigenhaus, a new setting for Liminal Horror I'm developing as part of this module. You could run an entire campaign around the Eigenhaus, and there could be more adventures for it coming.
