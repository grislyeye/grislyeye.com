Status: At Concept

Being a teenager on a zero-hour contract is tough, but then you get stuck in a remote gas station during a snowstorm. Just as shit starts to get weird.

Can you weather the night, or are you just another casualty of the gig economy?

An adventure for Liminal Horror and Liminal High School, in the genre of gig-work horror. Inspired by creepy-pasta, the KULT roleplaying game, John Dies at the End by David Wong, Hellraiser (1987), the Tales from the Gas Station podcast, the TV series Utopia and late-stage capitalism.
