They are not like us. Immortal, capricious, uncaring. Once they ruled our world, but now their magic dwindles as they retreat to their woods and caves.

The Fey is a more historical take on elves, dwarves, goblins and halfings. It includes new races, spells and monsters.

Due to concerns with the Wizard's of the Coast's licensing practices, I'm no longer continuing work on this product.
