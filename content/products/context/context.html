CONT/EXT is a one-shot of cosmic horror for use with Mothership®. A britpunk nightmare set aboard a failing research station.

You have been recruited by Continued Existence (CONT/EXT for short). CONT/EXT's mission is to ensure humanity can survive extinction-level threats, such as gamma radiation bursts or advanced, hostile aliens.

Rescue is optional

You've been sent to investigate the deep-space research station, the Lachesis. The station has been reporting system malfunctions and the crew have gone radio silent. It's up to you to retrieve the valuable research stored there.

Content Warnings: Claustrophobia, suicide, gore, mild references to pornography and drugs.

Purchase

Includes:

  • 12 pages of pure cosmic horror
  • a horrifying new threat
  • research station schematics
  • printable player map for table-top
  • colour player map for VTT
