As part of my eternal quest to seek out the best way to represent my NPCs I've been told monsters and NPCs should always be [doing stuff](https://www.paperspencils.com/structuring-encounter-tables/) when you encounter them.

So, what should they be doing exactly?

My first approach was to use activity tables: random lists of possible activities, customised for each NPC.

You can create activity tables for each monster or NPC. Spark tables are also great for this. With a bit of improvisation, you can frame encounters in interesting and unexpected ways, making them seem more alive.
