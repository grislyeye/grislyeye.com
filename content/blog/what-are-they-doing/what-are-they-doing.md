As part of my eternal quest to best represent NPCs in my games I've been told monsters and NPCs should be always be [doing stuff](https://www.paperspencils.com/structuring-encounter-tables/) when you encounter them. So what should they be doing exactly?

You can create activity tables for each monster or NPC. Spark tables are also great for this. With a bit of improvisation, you can frame encounters in interesting and unexpected ways making them seem more alive.
