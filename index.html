For centuries the vermus have lurked in the undercity sewers unbeknownst to the citizens above. But soon their wretched existence may be revealed…

This monograph will be part of Grisly Eye Games’ Threats Series. Each monograph will introduce new, setting-neutral monsters to supercharge your Dungeons & Dragons campaign.

Threats: Vermus introduces the vermus, a low-level monster for 1st to 2nd level characters. It includes a detailed description of the nature and culture of the vermus, 3x monster statistics (CR 1/4 to 1), campaign notes and adventure seeds.
